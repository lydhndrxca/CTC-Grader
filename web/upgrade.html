Upgrade Process

  1. Measure your specimen
    • Length (mm) - longest dimension
    • Width (mm) - perpendicular to length
    • Thickness (mm) - measured at thickest point
    • Weight (g) - on digital scale
  2. Re-photograph with ruler visible
    • Place ruler in frame for scale reference
    • Take high-resolution photos (1920x1920 or higher)
    • Include front, side, and back views minimum
    • Optional: Top-down, corner detail, surface close-ups
  3. Submit via Admin Portal
    • Upload all photos
    • Enter measurements manually
    • Confirm specimen ID (reference your Fan Edition submission)
  4. Receive 4-page official report
    • Page 1: Header & identification with measurements
    • Page 2: Subgrade analysis & curvature data
    • Page 3: Provenance & detailed interpretation
    • Page 4: System hash & appendix
