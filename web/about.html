About Multiview Technology

Purpose & Philosophy

Multiview Technology treats everyday cereal pieces with the analytical rigor of collectible grading. Each Cinnamon Toast Crunch specimen is documented and assigned a PSA-style rating using deterministic AI and geometric precision.

Grading Framework

Framework versions (v1.0–v1.5) refine weighting, curvature caps, and strict-mode enforcement to eliminate leniency. Geometry and curvature are core metrics; rounding always rounds down.

Technical Overview

  • AI detects geometry, curvature, and coating uniformity.
  • Manual mm/g measurements determine verification classification (MV/VO).
  • Curvature >7.5% automatically caps the maximum grade at 8.0.
  • All results are rounded down to the nearest 0.5 increment.

Archival Policy

Every report — valid, partial, or error — is preserved as an artifact. Provenance, framework version, and certification ID ensure total reproducibility. Lost or recovered files are documented as official "withholding" or "resolution" reports.
